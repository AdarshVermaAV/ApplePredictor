Upload and Predict Apple Type

Our Apple Predictor is a tool designed to identify different types of apples from your uploaded images. Simply upload a photo of an apple, and let our smart classifier tell you what type of apple it is.

Note: This tool is optimized for predicting apple types. If you upload an image of a different fruit, the prediction may be inaccurate.
